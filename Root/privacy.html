Privacy Policy

GamesWatcher was built by a team of one person, Giuliano Soria, an iOS developer located in British Columbia, Canada. All your data will be saved as security and safety was put first when developing the app.

All the saved information is either saved locally on device or on Apple's iCloud and Google's Firebase Services.

No third-party services are used to identify or track you as you use the app.

This website does not track you in any way or form, and does not ask for any personal information other than when you want to submit a message through the form down below.

If you have any questions or feedback of any kind, you are free to contact me through the form or via any of my Social Media accounts.
